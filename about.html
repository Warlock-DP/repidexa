About Rapidexa

Building the most reliable logistics network for regional businesses.

Our Mission

Founded in Vadodara, Rapidexa was built on a simple premise: regional logistics should be as reliable and transparent as national carriers, but with the flexibility that SMEs require.

We empower manufacturers, traders, and fast-growing brands across Gujarat and Western India by providing a supply chain backbone they can truly depend on.
